# Chorizo Stir Fry
## Ingredients
- 1/2 Chorizo
- Garlic
- Mushrooms/
- Noodles
- Eggs
- 30ml Light Soy sauce
- 30ml Dark Sor sauce
- 30ml Sweet Soy sauce, kepis manis
## Method
- Fry chorizo
- Fry garlic
- Fry noodles
- Add sauce
- Add veg
- Add eggs
- Serve
