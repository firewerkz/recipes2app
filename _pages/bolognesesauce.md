# Bolognese Sauce
## Ingredients
- Oil
- Onions
- Garlic
- Peppers
- Balsamic vinegar
- Sugar
- Mince
- Tinned Tomatoes
- Tomato pur√©e
- Worcestershire sauce
- Salt & Pepper to taste
## Method
- Fry onions, garlic and peppers
- Add sugar and vinegar
- Add mince and brown
- Add the rest and wash out any nearly empty sauce bottles or jars
- Simmer for 1 hour plus, the longer, the better
